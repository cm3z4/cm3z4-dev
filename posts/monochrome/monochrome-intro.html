Monochrome is more or less a personal experiment/project and an attempt to create a digital archive for my future self. Oddly, I'm fascinated by monochrome (back and white) photography and film. I could probably watch The Twilight Zone all day if I didn't have the equivalent of a child's attention span. I will attempt to post an indefinite amount of pictures per month at the end of every month. For the critics, I am not a professional photographer. I do, however, intend to experiment/learn during my journey as a hobbyist photographer. So without further ado, feel free to tag along.
